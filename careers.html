Corporate Culture

The foundation of our success starts with our commitment to employee satisfaction. Employees enjoy training programs for developing job skills as well as incentive programs that reward exceptional service. Status Energy recognizes the influence a positive work environment has on employee satisfaction so employees can expect a healthy work-life balance and easy communication between all levels of the organization. Our customers need partners with committed, long-term employees which is why we make employee satisfaction a top priority.
