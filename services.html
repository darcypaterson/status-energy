Services

Fluid Transportation

Our fluid transportation unit includes a fleet of modern trucks and tankers that operate 24/7, 365 days a year. The unit is staffed by safety certified, experienced drivers so you can feel confident knowing that your fluids will be handled professionally. We take safety and compliance seriously as is evident in our record of routinely surpassing safety inspections tests.

Service Providers and Partners

We are headquartered in the County of Grande Prairie and serve oil and gas producers within the region. Our employees are trained to meet and exceed industry standards for safety and compliance. Our fleet and equipment is serviced regularly and meets the highest standards for safety. If your project needs reliable and dependable oilfield services, talk to us.
