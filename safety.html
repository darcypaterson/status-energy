Safety

We Take Safety Seriously

At Status Energy, safety and compliance are our first priority. We ensure every employee knows, understands and follows industry standards for safety and compliance. As a result we've been selected as a preferred vendor with the best producers in Canada.

Our Clients

Status Energy is a proud partner with high-quality industry producers.

Industry Standards for Safety

We are SECOR certified and are registered with ISN and ComplyWorks. We perform monthly on-site safety evaluations and quarterly reviews on staff. We have incentive programs that reward employees for meeting or exceeding company performance evaluations. This combination of efforts means Status Energy has one of the best records for safety and compliance in the industry.


For more information on our safety and compliance programs, please contact our Safety Officer.
