In partnership with Horse Lake First Nation, Status Energy is a socially-responsible alternative to traditional oilfield services.

Status Energy supplies oil and gas producers in the region of Grande Prairie, Alberta with an alternative to traditional oilfield services. It is uniquely qualified to offer producers high-quality oilfield services along with an opportunity to demonstrate their commitment to social responsibility.

Organizational Goals


  •  Support employees with apprenticeship programs for tanking.
  •  Exceed industry standards for safety and compliance.
  •  Provide employment and economic opportunities to the members of the Horse Lake community.
  •  Through revenue generation, provide economic stability to Horse Lake First Nation.

Beyond Horse Lake First Nation

At Status Energy we demonstrate our commitment to Horse Lake First Nation as well as our employees, our community and our customers.

Our company provides a workplace that values its employees and ensures a healthy work-life balance. We believe the experience a positive workplace has on its employees extends outwards towards customers and community.

Through actions like community event sponsorship, we show our commitment to Horse Lake First Nation and to the larger community of Alberta and Canada.

Our success comes from our commitment to our customers. We continuously look for ways to improve efficiencies in operations to provide high quality, cost effective, experienced service to our customers.
